ABOUT US

OUR JOURNEY

HuddleUp kicked off its journey in 2024, when three passionate sports lovers had only one goal in mind: to create a community full of passionate sports players, ready to meet up and play their hearts out together. On our pitch, everyone is welcome to join the dream team! From those humble beginnings, HuddleUp has managed to build a community that's truly all about the thrill of the game, connecting people with fellow sports fans. No matter the sport you play, be it football, basketball, volleyball or something else, we’ve got a spot and a team for you. We’re all about creating special moments, both on and off the field, because we know it’s not just about the wins (although we love those) but also about the connection team players feel between them. So, huddle with us and you’ll have a guaranteed blast together with fellow sports fans!

THE NAME

Our name came from a simple question: what is something that brings all team players together? Is it the game itself? The sport? Well, it turned out to be something much more personal than that, something only a closely knit team would truly understand and value. Huddling together is what makes the team feel like a singular unit, something that brings all players together and connects them. “HuddleUp” is not only our name, but it is an invitation that we extend to all our users, we invite you all to huddle together and create your dream teams.

HUDDLEUP'S VISION

Sports is all about excitement, adrenaline pumping through your veins, and uniting communities. As fellow sports lovers we agree with that, and we believe that coming together and reigniting the thrill of the game makes sure divisions remain only in sport leagues. HuddleUP’s vision is of a world where this kind of mindset is just as present off the pitch as it is on the pitch.

OUR VALUES

Everyone Can Play

A player’s past should never define their future. Everyone is welcome to huddle up and find a new team to play with, no matter your background and experiences.

Thrill of the Game

The game should not only unite us but inspire us to show our passion for the sport. Experience with us the unimaginable heights of success and the heart-wrenching feeling of defeat.

Growing Together

There’s no I in Team, but there’s an I in WIN. Even if you build your own success, a good team environment is what fuels it. Connect with your teammates and make new friends with HuddleUp.
